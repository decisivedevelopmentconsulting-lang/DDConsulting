Frequently Asked Questions

❯ What makes your approach different from other consultants?

Most consultants offer recommendations. We embed ourselves fractionally and run the system alongside your team until you can operate it independently. We're measured by systems that work without us.

❯ How long does a typical Stabilisation Sprint take?

60–90 days. We map current state (2–3 weeks), design new operating system (2–3 weeks), then stabilise and train your team (4–6 weeks). You'll see measurable improvements by day 45.

❯ What if we don't have a clear delivery problem yet?

That's what the free diagnostic is for. We'll help you identify bottlenecks, assess delivery consistency, and clarify whether you're the right fit. No obligation if we're not a match.

❯ What's included in the diagnostic conversation?

We assess: current delivery metrics, team pain points, bottlenecks, founder time allocation, and growth challenges. We'll then recommend next steps — no pressure to engage if it's not right for you.

❯ Do you work with BPO firms specifically, or just recruitment?

We work with both. BPO firms face unique challenges around high-volume hiring, quality consistency, and retention. We've successfully stabilised BPO delivery operations across Southeast Asia.

❯ Can you work with firms outside Malaysia?

Yes. We work remotely with firms across Southeast Asia and beyond. We typically meet 2–3 times per week (2–3 hours) during engagements, with async work in between.
